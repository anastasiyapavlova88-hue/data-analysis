EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
This report analyzes smart device usage data from non-Bellabeat devices (Fitbit dataset) to identify behavioral trends and develop marketing strategy recommendations for Bellabeat.
Key findings show that users consistently track physical activity but engage far less with sleep and weight tracking features. The data also reveals strong correlations between physical activity and calories burned, but weak relationships between activity and sleep behavior. Most users do not reach recommended activity targets and show inconsistent engagement over time.
The main business opportunity identified is improving user retention and encouraging habit formation. Bellabeat can reposition its product from a fitness tracker to a daily wellness companion focused on habit-building, sleep improvement, and long-term engagement.
Key recommendations include:
’üČ	Habit-building onboarding and personalized goals 
’üČ	Stronger promotion of sleep tracking features 
’üČ	Gamification to increase engagement and retention 
’üČ	Segment-based marketing strategies 
Implementing these actions can increase user engagement, retention, and lifetime value.
